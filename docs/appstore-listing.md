## Description (4000)

```
ClawTV is a beautiful, fast IPTV player for Apple TV. You bring your own M3U playlist and (optionally) an XMLTV program guide; ClawTV does the rest.

NO BUNDLED CHANNELS
ClawTV ships with zero pre-loaded streams or playlists. It is a player only. You must already have a legitimate IPTV subscription, a personal NAS or Plex feed, or your own M3U source.

WHAT YOU GET
• Lightning-fast M3U parser handles playlists with tens of thousands of channels
• Full-screen Live TV with channel-up/down via the Siri Remote
• Multi-View: watch up to four streams at once with a focus-and-swap UX built for the Siri Remote
• Program guide (Now & Next) when you supply an XMLTV EPG
• Powerful search across channels, groups, and EPG titles
• Favorites and recently played, persisted on-device
• Settings to manage multiple playlists and EPG sources, refresh intervals, and stream preferences
• Built on the proven VLC engine for the widest stream-format compatibility

PRIVACY-FIRST
ClawTV collects nothing. No analytics, no crash reporters, no ads, no tracking, no servers. Your playlists and viewing data never leave your Apple TV. iCloud sync is intentionally not used.

7-DAY FREE TRIAL
Every feature is unlocked for 7 days from first launch. After the trial, unlock the app permanently with a one-time $9.99 in-app purchase. Buy once, own forever — no subscriptions.

REQUIREMENTS
• Apple TV HD or Apple TV 4K running tvOS 17.0 or later
• A legitimate M3U playlist URL (not provided)
• Optional XMLTV EPG URL for program guide

SUPPORT
support@clawtv.app

PRIVACY POLICY
https://clawtv.app/privacy
```

## Review notes (private to Apple reviewer)

```
Hello reviewer — thank you for your time.

ClawTV is a player-only application. It does NOT bundle, recommend, host, or distribute any video content. It opens M3U playlist URLs that the user enters themselves and plays the streams referenced inside.

To test the app, please paste this public sample playlist on the onboarding screen:
https://iptv-org.github.io/iptv/categories/news.m3u

This is a freely-licensed open-source list of news broadcasters that publish their streams openly. We do not include this URL in the app or in any marketing — it is provided here purely as a verification convenience for review.

The app shows a rights-attestation checkbox during onboarding. Users must affirm they have the right to access the streams they are about to load before they can proceed.

The 7-day free trial gates the full feature set. After expiry, the app shows a paywall with a single $9.99 non-consumable IAP (`com.clawtv.player.unlock`) and a Restore Purchase button. The IAP is configured in App Store Connect.

There is no account system, no server-side state, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs apart from the VLC media framework (LGPL). Privacy manifest declares zero data collection.

Demo / sandbox account: not required (no login).

Support contact: support@clawtv.app
```
