# ClawTV Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-25

ClawTV ("the app") is a tvOS application for playing IPTV streams that you supply yourself.

## What we collect

**Nothing.**

ClawTV does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. There is no analytics SDK, no crash-reporting service, no advertising network, and no remote logging.

## What stays on your device

Everything. The app stores the following data locally on your Apple TV only:

- The M3U playlist URLs you enter
- The XMLTV EPG (program guide) URLs you enter
- Your favorites, recently played channels, and view-state preferences
- Your in-app purchase status (managed by Apple's StoreKit)
- A local timer used to track your free-trial period

This data never leaves your Apple TV. It is not synced to iCloud and not transmitted to any server controlled by ClawTV or its developer.

## Network connections

ClawTV makes network requests only to:

1. **The playlist and EPG URLs you provide.** These are downloaded so the app can show you a channel list and program guide.
2. **The stream URLs contained in your playlist.** These are opened by the in-app player when you select a channel.
3. **Apple's App Store servers**, when you tap "Unlock" or "Restore Purchase". This is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework.

ClawTV has no servers of its own. We never see your playlist contents, your viewing habits, or your IP address.

## Third-party content

ClawTV is a generic player. It does not bundle, recommend, or distribute any video content. You are responsible for ensuring that the streams you load with ClawTV are ones you have a legal right to access.

## Children

ClawTV is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect any data from children or anyone else.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted at the same URL with an updated "Last updated" date.
