# ClawTV

A beautiful, native tvOS player for your IPTV and M3U playlists.

- [Privacy Policy](privacy.html)
- [Support](support.html)

## What is ClawTV?

ClawTV is a player. It does not provide, host, or sell any TV channels or content. You bring your own M3U playlist URL — typically from your IPTV provider, home media server, or self-hosted source.

## Features

- Beautiful native tvOS player built for the Siri Remote
- Built-in EPG / program guide from your own XMLTV source
- Multi-View — watch up to four channels at once
- Favorites, history, and instant search
- 7-day free trial, then $9.99 to unlock forever — no subscriptions
